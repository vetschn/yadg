Citing yadg

yadg is currently being developed at the ConCat Lab at Technische Universität Berlin, Berlin, Germany. It has been previously developed at Materials for Energy Conversion lab at Empa, Dübendorf, Switzerland. If you find yadg useful, please cite our recent paper [Kraus2022b], or our previous work where yadg was first introduced [Kraus2022a]. We extend no warranty, neither express nor implied.
